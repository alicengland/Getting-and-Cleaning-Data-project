# Code Book for the final project in the Coursera Getting and Cleaning Data course
#### These variables are relevant for both the merged training/test data set and the tidy data set

##### subject:
subject number = 1-30 (unique identifer that represents the volunteer)
    
##### activity:
activity performed while wearing the smartphone on the waist = walking, walkingupstairs, walkingdownstairs, sitting, standing, or laying
    
##### tBodyAcc-mean()-X:
mean value of X axial body acceleration signal, units of standard gravity (g)

##### tBodyAcc-mean()-Y:
mean value of Y axial body acceleration signal, units of standard gravity (g)

##### tBodyAcc-mean()-Z:
mean value of Z axial body acceleration signal, units of standard gravity (g)

##### tGravityAcc-mean()-X:
mean value of X axial gravity acceleration signal, units of standard gravity (g)

##### tGravityAcc-mean()-Y:
mean value of Y axial gravity acceleration signal, units of standard gravity (g)

##### tGravityAcc-mean()-Z:
mean value of Z axial gravity acceleration signal, units of standard gravity (g)

##### tBodyAccJerk-mean()-X:
mean value of jerk signal from X axial body acceleration signal derived in time, units of g/s

##### tBodyAccJerk-mean()-Y:
mean value of jerk signal from Y axial body acceleration signal derived in time, units of g/s

##### tBodyAccJerk-mean()-Z:
mean value of jerk signal from Z axial body acceleration signal derived in time, units of g/s

##### tBodyGyro-mean()-X:
mean value of X axial body angular velocity signal, units of radians/s

##### tBodyGyro-mean()-Y:
mean value of Y axial body angular velocity signal, units of radians/s

##### tBodyGyro-mean()-Z:
mean value of Z axial body angular velocity signal, units of radians/s

##### tBodyGyroJerk-mean()-X:
mean value of jerk signal from X axial body angular velocity signal derived in time, units of radians/s^2

##### tBodyGyroJerk-mean()-Y:
mean value of jerk signal from Y axial body angular velocity signal derived in time, units of radians/s^2

##### tBodyGyroJerk-mean()-Z:
mean value of jerk signal from Z axial body angular velocity signal derived in time, units of radians/s^2

##### tBodyAccMag-mean():
mean value of the magnitude of XYZ axial body acceleration signals (tBodyAcc-XYZ) calculated using the Euclidian norm, units of standard gravity (g)

##### tGravityAccMag-mean():
mean value of the magnitude of XYZ axial gravity acceleration signals (tGravAcc-XYZ) calculated using the Euclidian norm, units of standard gravity (g)

##### tBodyAccJerkMag-mean():
mean value of the magnitude of XYZ axial jerk body acceleration signals (tBodyAccJerk-XYZ) calculated using the Euclidian norm, units of g/s

##### tBodyGyroMag-mean():
mean value of the magnitude of XYZ axial body angular velocity signals (tBodyGyro-XYZ) calculated using the Euclidian norm, units of radians/s

##### tBodyGyroJerkMag-mean():
mean value of the magnitude of XYZ axial jerk body angular velocity signals (tBodyGyroJerk-XYZ) calculated using the Euclidian norm, units of radians/s^2

##### fBodyAcc-mean()-X:
